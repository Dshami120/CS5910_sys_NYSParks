About Parks & Recreation

Our Mission

To provide exceptional parks, recreation programs, and community services that enhance the quality of life for all residents. We are committed to preserving natural resources, promoting healthy lifestyles, and fostering community connections through accessible outdoor spaces and recreational opportunities.

Our History

Founded in 1950, the Parks & Recreation Department has been serving our community for over 70 years. What started as a small team maintaining a few local parks has grown into a comprehensive department managing hundreds of acres of parkland, recreational facilities, and community programs.

Throughout our history, we've remained dedicated to our core values: environmental stewardship, community engagement, and accessible recreation for all ages and abilities.
